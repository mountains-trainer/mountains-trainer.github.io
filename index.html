About me

The beginning of the way

The first trip is never forgotten, it is like a first date with a girl. You prepare for a long time, and then you either get everything or nothing. In my case, everything went at the best level, and I wanted more and more such meetings.

I began to prepare: to gather information, to go on hiking trips, to look at lectures on medicine and travel. And now with pride I can say I am the best in my business.

Activities - guide instructor;
Specialization - hiking;
Personal Achievements - Climbing Mount Kilimanjaro

Experience hikes - 15 years;
Guide instructor - 10 years;

routes

carpathians

The beginning of the ascent of the mountain begins at the base of the mountain, and continues until the end of the mountain. We make a halt on the road, all those who lag behind we do not wait for them, they are sent to be eaten by the wolves. Additional services, spending the night with an intruder in a tent, my love will be enough for everyone.

In the middle of our route, you can stay at the keeper Igor, warm up to buy up in the bathhouse and rub backs. Also for a fee you can eat well.

The route is worked out individually, there is a ready route.

Before going, you can consult with me about what to take with you from food.

Part or all of the equipment can be rented.

cost 7 days: 200$
